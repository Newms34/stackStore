Our Mission Statement

We brew coffee.
We make mints.
And we do it well. Want some fancy-schmancy pastries or one of those expensive chocolate doohickies that melt before you can even bite in to them?
Then go somewhere else.
But if you want mints that make you feel like a kid again, and coffee that makes you question what the brown goo you've previously been drinking all this time is, then come right in.

Our History

Coffee Meets Mint was founded in 1337 by a bunch of monks. Throughout history, our coffee has pushed the boundaries of caffinated beverages, and our mints have explored the very definition of refreshing.
